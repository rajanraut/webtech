Donate Blood, Save Lifes

Every day blood transfusions take place and save millions of lives all over the world.   However, there are still a number of countries who donot have adequate number of blood suppliers. Some countries are even relying mostly on blood from people that ask money for their gesture. It is alarming and saddening that things like this still happens.

Our Organization is trying to solve this problem of Blood Shortage.

Blood donation is a service to humankind. By donating blood, you help someone in need and it can very well save a precious life.

One of the main reasons why people are not donating blood is that some of them are not fully aware of its benefits and some are even afraid to do so because of misinformation. To better understand the concept, we have compiled some basic facts about blood donation.

The process is as simple as:

  1. Registration
  2. Mini-physical exam and medical history
  3. Donation of blood
  4. Refreshments

Our Team is helping people who are in urgent need of blood by providing information of Donors.
